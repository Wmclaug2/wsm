Who is Wes?

I was born and raised in Richmond VA where I attended the IB program for 7 years. I graduated and attended George Mason University for 2 years focusing on Technology and International Relations.After returning to RVA, I began working for Allianz Global Assistance, a multinational insurance corporation where I did basic troubleshooting and IT work alongside my core role for 1 1/2 years before moving to join Capital One working to detect credit card fraud. I excelled in this area but did not get to work with code/technology as much as I would have liked and knew that was where my passions lie. After accepting a job offer within the company, I worked to create a text analytics tool that has since been used in Partnerships with Uber and IHeartRadio along with a multitude of internal process. From here, I was able to establish my brand and become an advocate and instructor for new digital solutions within the organization.

Continuing on my journey, I remembered the Web Development work that I had done in college and began to get back into the field. After brushing up on my basics (HTML, CSS, JS) I went on to create a wide variety of sites for local bands, organizations and individuals. From here I hope to continue my journey as a Web Developer, grow my knowledge base and continue working within Tech.
